Camtasia Tips & Tricks

Quick Reference

Procedures

Recording Presentations

  1. Close everything that’s not necessary.
  2. Move everything on your desktop into a folder.
  3. Use a USB headset or nice microphone (Blue Snowball).
  4. Make sure audio/webcam is active before recording.

Editing

  1. Assemble your clips on the timeline.
  2. Drake the red and green markers next to the playhead to select the oopsie.
  3. Once you have the oopsie highlighted, CUT it (don’t use delete) to continue the rest of your video as if it never happened.

Embedding in Your Course

  1. Produce the file to your desktop as an mp4.
  2. In your course, choose Tools, Kaltura Media to launch Kaltura.
  3. In Kaltura, choose Add New, and then Media Upload.
  4. Upload the .mp4 file and then select it.
