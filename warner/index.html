TRAP Method Quick Reference

Timeliness

Is the instructional material current enough?

Relevance

Does the instructional material on the correct topic?
Is it age appropriate given your group?
Does it fit within the guidelines of your assignment?

Authority

Who created the material?
Do they have appropriate credentials to do so?

Purpose

Does the instructional material serve the correct purpose?
Does it help you meet the needs of your assignment?
Can the item be re-used/shared (creative commons licensing, etc.)?
